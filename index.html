Who We Are

A principal-led real estate firm built on unit-level economics — we underwrite location, tenant, and the cash flow an asset actually produces, not market momentum.

EquitiFy Capital is a privately held real estate investment and asset management firm focused on net-leased retail and fuel-retail commercial real estate across the United States. We acquire, develop, and manage single- and multi-tenant retail assets — with particular depth in convenience-store and fuel-retail properties.

Headquartered in the Dallas–Fort Worth area, we pair the speed and hands-on focus of a boutique firm with the underwriting discipline and risk management of an institutional platform.
